Online learning is both a challenge and a new opportunity to approach our mission of Caritas et Veritas. This page defines Dominican's five course modalities. It also provides reference materials from our Rebecca Crown Library and other universities to help assist you in designing online, hybrid, and face-to-face courses.

Course Modalities

Face-to-Face (F2F)
Course is scheduled on campus at set meeting times
Hybrid (HYB)
Course will include a combination of online activity and on-campus meeting times
Online with Fixed Class Meeting Times (OSM)
Course will have one or more scheduled online meetings; no campus attendance required
Online without Fixed Class Meeting Times (OWM)
Course will have no fixed scheduled class meeting times; no campus attendance required; consult syllabus for specific requirements
By Arrangement (ARR)
Time and setting to be arranged by instructor and student
